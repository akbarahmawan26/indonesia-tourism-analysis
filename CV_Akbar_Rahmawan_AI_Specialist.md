## Projects

### Indonesia Tourism Data Analysis
**GitHub:** github.com/akbarrahmawan26/indonesia-tourism-analysis

Analyzed 200+ Indonesian tourism records to identify visitor trends, revenue patterns, and destination performance metrics for government tourism planning.

- **Data Processing:** Generated and processed synthetic tourism data (8 features: visitors, revenue, satisfaction scores, marketing spend, weather, categories)
- **Key Findings:** 
  - Bali leads in visitor numbers (2.1M visitors)
  - Bandung generates highest revenue per visitor (2.9T IDR total)
  - March identified as peak tourism month
  - Strong correlation (0.81) between marketing spend and revenue
  - Domestic:International visitor ratio of 3:1
- **Visualizations:** Created 4-panel dashboard showing visitor distribution, revenue-satisfaction correlation, monthly trends, and category breakdowns
- **Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
